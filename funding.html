Funding and conflicts

The public site does not currently publish a funder list, donor policy, or conflict register. That status should remain visible until replaced by a fuller institutional disclosure.

Current status

Funder list pending Conflict register pending Decision rights pending

No public page should imply independent institutional trust until these disclosures exist.

Interim policy

Do not overclaim

Until funding and conflicts are public, the site should describe Normativity as a research project and prototype set, not as a fully disclosed public institution.

Make updates durable

Future funding updates should be dated, archived, and linked from governance, research, and pilot pages when they affect interpretation.

Normativity separates funding disclosures from product and demo claims.
