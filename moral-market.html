Interpretation: The board makes uncertainty visible. It does not prove that either answer is morally correct, and it should be read alongside reasons, not instead of them.

Key factors

Fair opportunity

Prestige proxies can amplify unequal access to elite credentials.

Predictive validity

Some screening processes may need evidence that proxies add value beyond direct skill measures.

Operational burden

Employers may need feasible alternatives, not only a prohibition.
