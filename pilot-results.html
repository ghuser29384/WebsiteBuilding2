Future pilot reporting template

Required field What future reports should disclose
Protocol Study question, participants, recruitment, consent, exclusion rules, and data collection dates.
Safeguards Privacy, retention, moderation, dispute review, revocation, and appeal procedures used.
Outcomes Predefined measures, uncertainty intervals, null results, adverse events, and limitations.
Materials Question prompts, example data labels, analysis code or summary, and correction history.

Readiness gates before publishing results

  • Named responsible parties and data owner are published.
  • Legal status, participant terms, and funding/conflict disclosures are published.
  • Data handling, export, deletion, retention, and revocation paths are tested.
  • Results distinguish measured outcomes from examples, anecdotes, and interface demos.

Normativity will distinguish measured pilot results from method demonstrations.
